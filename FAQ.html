Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions
How do I receive assistance?

Please email us at supportmy@paradise.com.


I have forgotten my password, what should I do?

Click the link 'Forgot password' here. Enter your email address, an eamil will be sent to guide you how to reset your password.


Do you sell second-hand book?

No. All of our books are brand new.


How do i locate a book?

Using our search bar on homepage, key-in book title or author or ISBN or keyword and then press 'search' button.



Payment
What are the available payment methods?

The modes of online payments that we accept are e-banking(FPX), Visa and MasterCards.



Return and Refund
Can I return an item?

Yes, return items are exchangeable but not refundable. Prior returning any items, please notify us at supportmy@paradise.com to discuss your reason for a return and we will organise an approval for the return and exchange. For more details, please read our Terms and Condition.


The books received was damaged or is not the one ordered?

We deeply apologised for the flaws and inconvenience caused. Please email us at supportmy@paradise within 2 days upon received your order. It may take approximately 5 business days for us to investigate and organise a refund or exchange for the damaged items. As soon as we identify the issues, store credits worth RM10 will be offered as a compensation.


How soon can i expect for a refund?

The refund and compensation process may take up to 7 business days.



Shipping
Do you ship overseas?

Unfortunately, we don't. Stay tuned for further notice.


When will I receive my order?

It will usually take only 3 to 5 business days for your books to reach you after the confirmation of order.

NOTE: Due to additional SOPs requirements relating to COVID-19 pandemic, do expect shipments delay.


How much will delivery cost?

1. Delivery charges will automatically be computed at the checkout page before proceed for payment.
2. Free shipping is applicable for Peninsular Malaysia with a minimum spend of RM150 or above in a single order. As for East Malaysia, there will be a 50% discount on shipping with a minimum spend of RM200 in a single order. Both of which will be shown automatically on your checkout page.


How do I track my order?

We will send an email with your tracking number to check the status of your order with the respective courier service company's website.


I haven't receive my order after a period of time. What should I do?

Please check with the relevant courier company with the tracking number given or by contacting us at supportmy@paradise with your details of order.



Technical Issues
I wasn't able to make payment. What should I do?

Clear your cache and cookies of the browser,login again to your account and click "My Orders" to check the status of your order. If it is empty, click your cart and proceed with payment again. If the issue persist, do not hesitate to contact us at supportmy@paradise.


I wasn't able to load the webpage and encountered error at 'Add to Cart', 'Checkout' or etc. What should I do?

Try enabling the cookies usage and refresh the browser. Try not to use browser in private mode,"Chrome Incognito for example" as it blocks any third-party cookies.


I have other issues, how to I get help?

Feel free to email us at supportmy@paradise for further assistance.
